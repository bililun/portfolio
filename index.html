Hi! I'm PK or Lauren, either works (they/them). I love making and playing games because they blend art and fun like nothing else. I'm especially fond of fast-paced strategy games, narrative-heavy or interactive fiction games, and RPGs.

I'll be graduating from Harvard College in 2025 with a degree in Computer Science in the Mind Brain Behavior honors track (interdisciplinary emphasis on cognition and neuroscience). In my time here, I've held leadership positions in the Harvard Game Developers Club, Harvard-Radcliffe Dramatic Club, and Radcliffe Choral Society, while maintaining a strong academic track record across rigorous coursework in computer science, applied math, and creative writing.

I'm experienced in Unity, Godot, Ink, Twine, and Love2D, and I program in C++, C#, Python, Java, Swift/Xcode, Processing, Javascript, and HTML.

projectsA sampling of my recent projects:

Ultimate

In-browser Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe with a glitch-distortion twist (2023). Designed and programmed by me using Godot. Ultimate was a passion project inspired by a scrap-paper game among friends and motivated because I wanted to improve my skill with Godot.

We Are Free Mice

Lead programmer, and writer, for Zero Summer Studios' upcoming narrative tarot-playing game of mice and antiquity. I have designed and implemented new features in Unity, maintained our codebase, and written narrative in ink.

Witchlight

In-browser spooky platformer (2022). Contributed to concept, level design and code (notably, movement and the potion minigame). Made in Unity for a project-based course "Creating Video Games."

Lucid Dreaming

In-browser interactive fiction about lucid dreaming (2021). Written and programmed by me using Twine for a seminar titled "Dreams: Our Mind by Night."
